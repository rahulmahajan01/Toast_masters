Where We Stand Today

Ever since the club was founded in date. Thapar Toastmasters has come a long way in not only training its members to be better speakers, evaluators and listeners. Rather, it has set new benchmarks in the area and division level. It can be clearly seen in the awards and recognition we have gotten over the years.

The Student Activity Award

The Student Activity Awards was given to us as we excelled to help students grow and for displaying immense growth.

The Enterprising Club Award

The Enterprising Club Award was given by Division B as we helped conduct mock meetings in various clubs.

The Accelerators Award

The Accelerators Award was given to the club as it managed to add a minimum of 15 new members by 30th April.

The Distinguished Ahead of Time Award

The Ahead of Time Award was given to us as we achieved a certain number of DCP goals ahead of the given time.
